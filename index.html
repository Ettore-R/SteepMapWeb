Everything for your day out

One app, from first idea to final turn

Steep brings discovery, planning and your community together - so you spend less time guessing and more time in the good snow.

Explore the backcountry

Discover tours you never knew existed

Pan across the map and watch tours light up - from mellow valley tours to committing alpine couloirs. Every line comes with photos, stats and the story of who's skied it, on desktop and in your pocket.

  • Browse an interactive map packed with real tours
  • Filter by vertical, difficulty and region
  • See conditions and photos from people who went
Ski with your crew

Repeat the tours your friends have skied

Your feed shows what your friends have been skiing. Loved the look of that descent? Open it, save it, and repeat it yourself - with the same track, stats and beta they used.

  • A live feed of your friends' latest tours
  • Repeat any tour with one tap
  • React, comment and plan the next one together
Plan like a pro

Read the mountain before you're on it

Toggle map overlays to understand the terrain from your couch. Combine slope steepness and aspect with satellite and topo basemaps to plan a smart, safe route up and down.

Overlays are part of SteepMap Pro - everything else is free to explore.

Your bucket list

Found a tour that makes your heart race? Save it to your bucket list with a tap, come back when the conditions align, and tick it off once you've earned the turns.
