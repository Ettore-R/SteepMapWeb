Plan like a pro

Read the mountain before you're on it

Toggle map overlays to understand the terrain from your couch. Combine slope steepness and aspect with satellite and topo basemaps to plan a smart, safe route up and down.

Slope steepness

A colour-graded overlay reveals exactly how steep each pitch is - spot the 30–45° zones that matter most for your line and your safety margin.

Slope aspect

See which way every slope faces at a glance. Aspect drives sun, snowpack and hazard - so you can chase the powder and avoid the wrong side of the mountain.

Satellite, topo & terrain

Switch between crisp satellite imagery, detailed topographic maps and a shaded terrain view - whatever helps you picture the day ahead.

Overlays are part of SteepMap Pro - everything else is free to explore.
